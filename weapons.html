Valorant Guns

Pistols

The Classic is a versatile pistol with a reliable fire rate. It excels at close-range combat.

The Shorty is a compact shotgun, effective in close quarters for quick kills.

The Frenzy is a full-auto pistol, delivering rapid fire with a high rate of fire.

The Ghost is a silent pistol with high damage, ideal for stealthy engagements.

The Sheriff is a powerful revolver, capable of one-shot headshots at medium range.

SMGs

The Stinger is a fast-firing SMG with high damage at close range but low accuracy at distance.

The Spectre is a high-mobility SMG, offering solid damage at close range with a fast rate of fire.

Rifles

The Bulldog is a versatile, semi-automatic rifle, great for mid-range engagements with a burst fire option.

The Guardian is a semi-automatic rifle with high damage and low recoil, ideal for long-range precision.

The Phantom is a fully automatic rifle, known for its high fire rate and accuracy at medium range.

The Vandal is a powerful automatic rifle, dealing high damage with a consistent accuracy across all ranges.

Shotguns

The Bucky is a pump-action shotgun, perfect for close-quarters combat with high damage potential.

The Judge is a fully automatic shotgun, great for up-close engagements with a high rate of fire.

Sniper Rifles

The Marshal is a one-shot sniper rifle, ideal for long-range picks with high accuracy.

The Operator is a high-damage sniper rifle capable of killing with a single shot to the chest or head.
